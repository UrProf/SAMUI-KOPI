About Us

Samui Kopi is a Japanese name that means "cold coffee" that is typically made by brewing hot coffee and then cooling it down by adding ice or refrigerating it. It can also be made using a "flash brew" method, where hot coffee is brewed directly into ice to prevent dilution. Samui Kopi is a cup of clean, consistent, and well-handled coffee. With the best quality ingredients, high-level coffee brewing technology, and an innovative business model, we are evolving the concept of coffee consumption to make specialty coffee affordable for everyone, every day.

From innovative menus crafted with care to the warmth of our hospitality, every detail reflects our passion for making every occasion special.

In 2024, we proudly expanded beyond Metro Manila to bring our unique dining experiences to more communities. By 2025, we aim to share the Samui Kopi story across Luzon, and by 2026, we plan to celebrate wins beyond the Philippines, introducing our world-class hospitality to a global audience.
